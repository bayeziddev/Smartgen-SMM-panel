For any privacy-related questions or requests, you can contact us directly through our Contact Us page or via WhatsApp as listed there.

What Information We Collect

We only collect information that is genuinely necessary to provide our services, process your payments, and support your account. Here is a clear breakdown of what that includes:

Information You Provide Directly

When you register for a SMMSun account, you provide us with your email address and a password of your choosing. This is the minimum information required to create and secure your account.

When you place an order, you provide the public URL or username of the social media account or content you want to boost. We want to be very clear here: we collect the link or username you enter into our order form. We do not collect, store, or have any access to your social media login credentials. We never ask for your Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, or any other platform password.

When you contact our support team via WhatsApp or email, we retain the content of that communication to help us resolve your issue and improve our service.

Payment Information

SMMSun does not store your full payment details on our servers. When you add funds to your account using bKash, Nagad, Rocket, Upay, Cellfin, Visa, Mastercard, Binance Pay, or Payeer, the transaction is processed through each respective payment provider's own secure system. We receive confirmation of the transaction and the amount, but we do not see or store your full mobile banking PIN, card number, or wallet credentials.

We do retain a record of your transaction history on SMMSun for accounting purposes and to help resolve any payment disputes. This record includes the amount, date, payment method type, and transaction reference number.

Information Collected Automatically

Like most websites, SMMSun automatically collects certain technical information when you visit or use our platform. This includes your IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, the pages you visit on our site, how long you spend on each page, and the links you click.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

SMMSun uses cookies on our website. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help our platform remember your login session, your language preferences, and other settings that make your experience smoother. You can control cookie settings through your browser, but disabling them may affect platform features.

How We Use Your Information

Every piece of information we collect is used for a specific, legitimate purpose. Here is exactly what we use your data for:

  • To Create and Manage Your Account: To verify your identity when you log in, and communicate with you about your account activity.
  • To Process and Deliver Your Orders: The social media URL/username is used solely to deliver the service you purchased. We do not use your order links for any other purpose.
  • To Process Payments and Maintain Financial Records: To maintain your account balance, issue refunds, and resolve billing disputes in compliance with accounting regulations.
  • To Provide Customer Support: To understand and resolve your issues effectively when you contact us.
  • To Improve Our Platform: Aggregated analytics help us understand how our platform is being used to improve the overall experience.
  • To Keep Our Platform Secure: IP addresses and technical logs are used to detect and prevent fraudulent activity and unauthorized access.
  • To Communicate Service Updates: To send important service communications (not marketing emails) regarding changes to the platform.

Who We Share Your Information With

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your personal data with advertisers. We do not trade your information with third parties for their own marketing purposes. Data is only shared in the following limited situations:

Payment Processors: Necessary transaction data is shared securely with providers like bKash, Nagad, Visa, etc., to fulfill your deposits.

Service Delivery Partners: The public URL or username you provide is shared with our delivery network for fulfilment purposes only. They do not receive your name or email.

Analytics Tools: Standard web tools may collect anonymized, aggregated data about site visits without personally identifiable information.

Legal Requirements & Business Transfers: We will disclose information if required by law or government authority in Bangladesh. Data may also be transferred if SMMSun is acquired or restructured.

Data Storage, Security, and Retention

SMMSun takes the security of your personal data seriously. We store passwords in encrypted form and never in plain text. Payment transactions are handled through secure third-party infrastructures; we do not store full payment credentials on our servers.

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary. Your account data is retained while active. Transaction records are retained as required by financial regulations in Bangladesh. Technical logs are typically kept for 90 days before deletion or anonymization.

If you ever suspect unauthorized access to your SMMSun account, contact our support team immediately.

Your Rights Over Your Personal Data

You can request a summary of the data we hold or request corrections to outdated information by contacting support.

Right to Deletion

You have the right to request full deletion of your personal data and account, subject to legal record-keeping obligations.

Right to Withdraw Consent

You may withdraw consent for data processing at any time, without affecting prior lawful processing.

Right to Complain

If unsatisfied with our data practices, you may raise a complaint with our team or relevant data protection authorities in Bangladesh.

SMMSun is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

Our website may contain links to third-party platforms (like Facebook, TikTok, payment gateways). SMMSun is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites. Their own privacy policies apply when you leave our site.

Got Questions About Privacy?

If you have specific questions about how your data is handled on SMMSun, find your answers below.

Does SMMSun sell my personal data to anyone? ▼
No. SMMSun does not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to any third party for any purpose. The limited sharing described in this policy is restricted to payment processing, service delivery, and legal compliance only.
Is my bKash or Nagad payment information stored by SMMSun? ▼
No. We do not store your full payment details, PIN, or wallet credentials. Transactions are processed securely by the respective providers. We only receive confirmation of the transaction and amount for accounting purposes.
Is my data safe on SMMSun? ▼
Yes. We take security seriously, storing passwords in encrypted forms and utilizing secure infrastructures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration.
Does SMMSun ever ask for my social media passwords? ▼
Never. We only require the public URL or username of the account or post you want to boost. If anyone claiming to be from SMMSun asks for your password, it is fraudulent.
Can I delete my SMMSun account and all my data? ▼
Yes, you have the right to request that we delete your personal data and close your account by contacting our support team, subject to any legal transaction record-keeping requirements.
What data does SMMSun collect when I just visit the website? ▼
Like most websites, we automatically collect basic technical data (IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited) via standard analytics tools and cookies to keep the platform secure and improve user experience.
